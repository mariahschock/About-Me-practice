The Great Predators Among Us

Fiercely independent, domesticated cats have coexisted with humans for more than 10.000 years. But, who within this relationship, is truly in charge?
Some experts may say that the larger beings, the humans, are clearly the ones with all the power. However, these "experts", clearly don't live with the beloved felines themselves. For if they did, they would recognize that it is indeed, the cat who obtains the power.
It is known that grown cats do not meow at other grown cats to communicate. No, this is something that they developed to communicate with humans...Some may even say, to manipulate humans, especially when it comes to getting something they want.

Click the buttons below to find out how Leo, TK, and Phoebe, (The Babes), have all been master manipulators in the lives of their humans.

Who is in charge? Leo.
